Alain Pagani

I am a Principal Researcher at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), and Deputy Head of the Augmented Vision department.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Image Understanding, Augmented Reality and Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in 3D Computer Vision and Image-based scene reconstruction as well as in Object Detection and Tracking.

I am involved in publicly founded research projects as well as in industrial application projects.
